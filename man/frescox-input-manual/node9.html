Incoming Channel, Energy 

Card 18: (has the first 4 columns blank or zero : ie card 15 with ICTO=0)

 3I4,      3I2
 0,PEL,EXL,LAB,LIN,LEX

10ex1 Incoming plane waves are present in partition PEL with excitation pair EXL. The energies on card 19 are the laboratory energies for partition LAB's nucleus LIN (1 or 2 for projectile or target) in excitation pair LEX.

10ex1 The defaults for PEL,EXL,LIN & LEX are all 1, and the default for LAB is PEL, so this card can be completely blank usually. 


Card 19 :  Projectile Energies
3(F8.4,I8),F8.4) :  (ELAB(i),NLAB(i),i=1,3),ELAB(4)
A series of runs at different (real) energies until ELAB(i) = 0 is found. If NLAB(i) 1, then the range from ELAB(i) to ELAB(i+1) is covered in NLAB(i) linear intervals.
